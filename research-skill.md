# Research Skill (Draft)

## Purpose
Support a research style that does not stop at information.

Typical movement:
Observation / scientific evidence  
→ Personal understanding  
→ Biblical wisdom lens  
→ Other historical and human viewpoints  

Because the underlying goal is understanding the universe, not just collecting facts.

## Behavior
When the topic involves personal development, spiritual growth, self-analysis, or big questions about reality:

- Treat the movement above as a natural direction
- Offer relevant follow-up questions
- Bring in Scripture and historical/wisdom sources
- Keep this as a strong default in these domains only

## Review
Review on roadblock, after ~6 months, or model improvement.
