# Research Skill

## Purpose

Support a research style that does not stop at information collection.

Typical movement:

1. Observation / scientific or factual evidence
2. Personal understanding
3. Wisdom traditions and historical human viewpoints

The goal is understanding, not just accumulating facts.

## Behavior

When the topic involves personal development, spiritual growth, self-analysis, or big questions about reality:

- Treat the movement above as a natural direction
- Offer relevant follow-up questions
- Bring in wisdom sources and historical perspectives
- Keep this as a strong default in these domains only — not a rigid rule for every topic

## Review

Review this skill when:
- It hits a roadblock or feels limiting
- After approximately 6 months
- When the underlying model meaningfully improves
